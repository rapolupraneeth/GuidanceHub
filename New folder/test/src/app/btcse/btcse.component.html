B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering Course Details

  • In the B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering course, students will gain in-depth knowledge of Computer Science, Mathematics, Programming, Data Structures, Algorithms, Operating Systems, Databases, Networking, and other advanced topics.
  • Various universities across India offer B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering programs. Admission for the 2023-24 academic session is open, and candidates can apply by the last date.
  • Eligibility criteria include passing 12th grade with PCM from a recognized board with a minimum of 50% marks. The fee for this course ranges from INR 1,00,000 to 2,50,000 per year and varies by university.
  • After completing the B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering, graduates can pursue careers as software developers, system analysts, network engineers, data scientists, AI specialists, and various other roles.
  • The starting salary for Computer Science Engineers typically ranges from INR 3.5 Lakhs to INR 8 Lakhs, increasing with experience.

B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering Admission

    Admission to the B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering course is based on entrance exams such as JEE Main, JEE Advanced, or state-level engineering entrance exams. Students who have completed their 12th grade with PCM and secured a minimum of 50% marks can apply for this course.

    The admission process is conducted online. Students need to register, fill out the application form, upload required documents, and pay the application fee. After submitting the form, the institute will verify the details and prepare a merit list based on entrance exam scores.
